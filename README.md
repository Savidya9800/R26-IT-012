✨ We are excited to present our project! ✨

🚀 CardioGastro AI is an intelligent healthcare support system developed as part of our Research project.
Our solution focuses on accurately distinguishing between gastric-related chest pain and heart attack conditions, helping to reduce misdiagnosis and improve early medical decision-making.

💡 Project Overview

Chest pain is a common symptom that can be misleading, often causing confusion between gastric issues and serious cardiac conditions like heart attacks.

🔍 Our system integrates multiple diagnostic inputs to provide a reliable assessment:

📋 Patient Questionnaire Analysis
🧪 Troponin Test Results
📈 ECG (Electrocardiogram) Reports
🫀 Echo (Echocardiogram) Results
🔬 Angiogram Findings
⚙️ Key Features

🎯 Our Goal

To assist healthcare professionals and patients by providing a smart, fast, and reliable decision-support system that minimizes risk and enhances diagnostic accuracy.
